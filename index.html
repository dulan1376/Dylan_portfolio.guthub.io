Mabisatalk Mobile Application Offline Translation

MabisaTalk, a mobile app nestled in the Romblon archipelago, Philippines, tackles communication barriers for both tourists and locals. It offers a dual solution seamless translation: Navigating vibrant trails, interacting with friendly locals, and experiencing cultural immersion without language hurdles. Onhan language preservation: Dedicated learning modules and programs safeguard the endangered dialect, empowering individuals to learn and appreciate its unique beauty. Built with offline functionality and continuous improvement principles, MabisaTalk caters to two audiences: Tourists fostering smoother navigation and deeper cultural connections. Onhan language learners: Preserving the dialect and fostering cross-cultural understanding. MabisaTalk, bridge the linguistic gap, and discover the magic of Romblon, one conversation at a time.




An IOT-based 3D LED art

Project 3D LED Art is an interactive art piece that uses 64 tiny LED lights to create colorful and ever-changing patterns. Algorithms control the lights, generating mesmerizing displays. This project takes things further by incorporating Internet of Things (IoT) technology, allowing the art to respond to external stimuli and creating an immersive experience. It showcases how technology and art can merge to create dynamic and captivating masterpieces.
